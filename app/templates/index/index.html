Welcome to the MYO-xIA application

Context:

This application was developed within the 80-Prime MYO-xIA project.. The MYO-xIA project aims to collect data from patients with congenital myopathies in order to analyze them using explainable AI approaches. This application is intended to complete the missing functions of the patient management software: Phenotips.
In the field of congenital myopathies research, there is an important need for digitization and standardization of patient data in order to use modern data analysis methods.

Functionalities:

Two functionalities are now available:

  • Annotation of histological images: allows to record and annotate images of histological sections of tissues of patients with congenital myopathy. It is possible to delimit areas on the images and to associate them with labels. It is also possible to produce a histological report with a list of absence/presence of histopathological features and to associate a diagnosis with it.

  • Optical character recognition (OCR-PDF): Allows you to save and automatically convert PDF files to text format. This makes it easier to scan and retrieve data from correspondence between patient, doctor and biologist.

How to use the application:

  1. Prerequisites: Register the patient with general information, pedigree, phenotype and genetic diagnosis on the Phenotips application available here: https://www.lbgi.fr/phenotips
  2. Login to your MYO-xIA account in the top right corner
  3. Click in the top left menu on "Image Annot" to annotate histological sections or "OCR PDF" to scan a PDF file
  4. For both features it is necessary to fill in a form with the patient ID, first and last name. In terms of ID, the phenotips identifier must be used to link the two applications.
  5. Let yourself be guided by what is displayed on the screen.
